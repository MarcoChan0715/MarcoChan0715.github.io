Keyboard Hobby

Building a keyboard is similar to LEGO, choose your favourite pieces and build it together. For custom keyboards, you can choose your own mecahnical switches, keycaps, texture for the keyboard. Each choice brings different effects to the keyboard, for example, smoother typing experience, improving typing sounds and incerease of typing speed! The most important thing is you are able to get creative to personalise your own unique keyboard. The downside of this hobby is quite costly. The basic one cost around £150.
